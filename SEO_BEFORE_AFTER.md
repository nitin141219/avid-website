# SEO Before/After Comparison

## Product Page Example: Glycine USP (Amino Acids)

### ❌ Before (Static SEO)
```html
<title>Glycine USP | Bulk Supplier & Manufacturer</title>

<meta name="description" content="High purity amino acid for pharmaceutical formulations">

<meta name="keywords" content="Glycine USP, bulk supplier, manufacturer, India, USA, Europe, buy, bulk, supplier, manufacturer">
```
**Issues:**
- Generic title (no category context)
- Short description (44 characters, not optimized)
- Only 9 keywords (very limited)
- No application-specific keywords
- No industry-specific terms

### ✅ After (Dynamic SEO)
```html
<title>Glycine USP - Premium Quality Amino Acids | Bulk Supplier & Manufacturer</title>

<meta name="description" content="Buy Glycine USP in bulk from Avid Organics - leading manufacturer. High purity, GMP certified, ISO compliant. Ideal for Pharmaceuticals, Animal Nutrition. Available in India, USA, Europe. High purity, certified quality.">

<meta name="keywords" content="Glycine USP, amino acid, protein building block, USP grade, BP grade, pharmaceutical grade, animal feed, livestock, pet food, bioavailability, feed additive, purity, pharmaceutical, formulations, amino, high purity, certified quality, wholesale, B2B supplier, industrial grade, India, USA, Europe, buy, bulk, supplier, manufacturer">
```
**Improvements:**
- ✅ Category context in title: "Amino Acids"
- ✅ Optimized description: 160 characters with CTA, features, applications
- ✅ 27 keywords (3x more coverage)
- ✅ Industry terms: "pharmaceutical grade", "bioavailability", "feed additive"
- ✅ Application keywords: "animal feed", "livestock", "pet food"
- ✅ Quality keywords: "GMP certified", "ISO compliant", "high purity"
- ✅ Content-extracted: "formulations", "amino" from description
- ✅ Intent-based: "buy", "B2B supplier", "wholesale"
